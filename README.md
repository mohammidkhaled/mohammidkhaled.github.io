2. Privacy Policy
   Last Updated: 23/12/2025

Clad ("we," "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your style and shopping data.

## Information We Collect
Profile Data: Name, email, saved sizes (including custom measurements), style preferences (e.g., "minimalist," "bold"), fit notes, and wish lists.

Interaction Data: How you engage with style quizzes, which outfits you save, items you zoom in on, and virtual try-on sessions (note: we do not store body scan images).

Transaction Data: Purchase history, returns (including reasons for return like "too small" to improve fit algorithms), and payment information processed securely by our partners.

Technical Data: Device type, IP address, and app performance data.

How We Use Your Information
Core Service: To process orders, manage returns, and provide customer support.

Style Personalization: To power "Your Clad Digest," "Fit Profile," and "Recommended For You" sections.

App Improvement: To refine our size prediction engine using aggregated, anonymous data from returns and fit feedback.

Communication: To send order updates, style tips, and (with your consent) promotional emails about new collections matching your taste.

Sharing of Information
Brands & Designers: When you purchase, we share necessary order details for fulfillment. We may share aggregated fit feedback (e.g., "30% of customers sized down in this brand") to help them improve.

Service Providers: With logistics partners, payment processors, and analytics services under strict confidentiality.

Legal Compliance: When required by law.

We do not sell your personal data.

Your Choices & Rights
Access, update, or delete your profile data in Settings > Profile.

Adjust your style preferences and privacy settings at any time.

Opt out of marketing emails via the unsubscribe link.

Request data portability for your style profile and purchase history.

Data Retention
We retain your data while your account is active. You can request account deletion, after which we anonymize transaction data for legal records and aggregated analytics.

Contact
For privacy concerns: privacy@clad.style
